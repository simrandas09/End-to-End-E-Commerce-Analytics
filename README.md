# 🛒 End-to-End E-Commerce Business Performance & Growth Analytics using SQL Server & Power BI

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing end-to-end business performance of an E-Commerce platform using real-world transactional data from the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset. The objective is to identify customer behavior patterns, conversion bottlenecks, retention trends, churn risk, and high-value customer segments using Business Analysis and Product Analytics techniques.

This project demonstrates the practical application of:

* Business Requirement Gathering
* KPI Framework Design
* Customer Segmentation (RFM)
* Funnel Analysis
* Cohort Retention Analysis
* Churn Analysis
* Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
* Revenue Analysis
* Executive Dashboarding

---

## 🎯 Business Problem Statement

An e-commerce platform has observed declining customer retention and inconsistent conversion rates across different marketing channels. The leadership team wants to:

* Identify drop-off points in the purchase journey
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Segment high-value customers
* Measure customer retention and churn
* Optimize revenue-driving channels
* Improve Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

## 🔍 Business Analysis Performed

### 1️⃣ Conversion Funnel Analysis

Analyzed customer journey stages:

* Order Approved
* Order Shipped
* Order Delivered

Identified drop-offs between stages to detect fulfillment inefficiencies.

---

### 2️⃣ Customer Segmentation (RFM Analysis)

Customers were segmented based on:

* Recency
* Frequency
* Monetary Value

Segments Identified:

* Champions
* Loyal Customers
* Potential Loyalists
* At Risk Customers
* Lost Customers

---

### 3️⃣ Cohort Retention Analysis

Tracked customer return behavior over time using monthly cohorts.

This analysis helps:

* Measure engagement
* Understand loyalty trends
* Detect retention drop-offs

---

### 4️⃣ Customer Churn Analysis

Customers with no purchase in the last 90 days were marked as churned.

This helps:

* Identify inactive users
* Design retention campaigns
* Improve customer lifecycle management

---

### 5️⃣ Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

Calculated the total revenue generated per customer to:

* Identify high-value segments
* Optimize marketing investments
* Improve targeting strategies

---

### 6️⃣ Payment Channel Performance

Analyzed revenue contribution by payment method to:

* Understand purchasing behavior
* Detect high-value payment preferences
* Optimize checkout strategies

## 💡 Key Insights

* Significant drop-off observed between shipping and delivery stages
* Loyal customers contribute majority of total revenue
* Retention declines after 2nd month for most cohorts
* A large proportion of users churn within 90 days
* Credit card users generate highest revenue share

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Improve logistics efficiency to reduce delivery delays
* Target "At Risk" customers using personalized retention campaigns
* Focus marketing on high CLV customer segments
* Optimize checkout flow for low-performing payment types
* Implement loyalty programs to increase repeat purchases
